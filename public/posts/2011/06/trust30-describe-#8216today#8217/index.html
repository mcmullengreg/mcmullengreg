trust30: Describe ‘Today’

Today’s task seems slightly more daunting than yesterday. Somehow I am supposed to describe my day in only one sentence. Shouldn’t be too hard…right? Author Liz Danzico.

“Your genuine action will explain itself, and will explain your other genuine actions. Your conformity explains nothing. The force of character is cumulative. – Ralph Waldo Emerson, Self-Reliance”

My Response: Don’t let today get in the way of the rest of your life; you’ll have struggles but keep your friends and family close, they’re the best support you’ll ever need.

Some people reading this may not like Eminem or his music, however, the song Lose Yourself from 8 Mile is extremely powerful to me. That’s the reason why I have decided to post that video alongside this post.
